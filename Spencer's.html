Spencer's Emails

Engage, connect, and inform constituents. Create CTA's to the site to raise conversion rate.

Project Details

Design engaging emails to be received across the nation.

I embarked on a creative journey to design captivating email campaigns that showcase an array of body jewelry and other products, aimed at captivating a wide and diverse audience. With a focus on keeping the Spencer's branding, my designs are imbued with many colors and vivid imagery, meticulously chosen to resonate with the demographic's preferences and aspirations.

My Goals

  • Diversify Typography: I aimed to diversify the typography within the emails to make each communication piece stand out. By experimenting with various fonts, sizes, and styles, I ensured that the textual elements complement the overall design and effectively convey the message.
  • Simplify Categories: Recognizing the importance of ease of navigation and clarity, I streamlined the categories of body jewelry. This simplification allows recipients to effortlessly browse through the offerings, enhancing their engagement and interaction with the content.
  • Use Logical Groupings: The arrangement of products and information into logical groupings within the emails ensures a coherent and intuitive user experience. This thoughtful organization aids the viewer's journey through the content, making the exploration of each product both enjoyable and straightforward.

Implementation and Creative Process

In bringing this vision to life, I took an active role in creating the perfect backdrop for our photoshoots on Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, which were crucial in capturing the essence of the jewelry and the mood we aimed to convey. Being present at the photoshoots allowed me to observe the models up close, witnessing firsthand the interplay of light, shadow, and color on the jewelry pieces. This experience informed my design choices, enabling me to select images that would not only complement but enhance the email designs.

Leveraging these carefully chosen images, I designed my graphics to weave seamlessly around them, ensuring that each email not only showcased the jewelry but also told a story. The main text "bling," bold and unmissable, serves as a central theme, inviting the audience to explore how adding a little sparkle and shine can transform their look.

Through a blend of strategic typography, simplified categorization, and logical groupings, paired with the visuals from our photoshoots, the email campaigns are designed to engage, inspire, and captivate. The goal is not just to present the products but to offer an experience that speaks to the uniqueness of the products as well.
